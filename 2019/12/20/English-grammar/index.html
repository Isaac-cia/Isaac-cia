英语语法错题

发表于 2019-12-20 | 分类于 英语 | 次阅读

111

  1. Three months is not enough for the team to accomplish this task.
  2. To give more attention, the trees could have grown better. (not given)
  3. The doctor did a lot to reduce the patient’s fear that he would die of the disease. (not which?)
  4. It worried her a bit that her hair was turning gray.(not if?)
  5. The only language that is easy to learn is the mother tongue. (not which)
  6. Is this the factory which | that a lot of students visited yesterday? (not “to which”)
  7. If she doesn’t want to go hiking tomorrow, B.
    A. nor do I B. neither shall I C. neither do I D. I don’t neither
  8. The actor threw himself from the horse, as if C.
    A. to be shot B. being shot C. shot D. shooting
